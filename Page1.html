Welcome to The Wolf Game

Welcome! In this game you will create a wolf pack and help it thrive by breeding the wolves, welcoming new wolves to the pack, and much more. Each time you make it to the next generation, you will have more options for the pack. However, each generation will get more challenging. make sure pack thrives! There is a maximum of 10 wolves you can let into your pack. You can choose who to let in and who you kick out. The maximum number will increase when your pack level increases

there are three roles. Click a button to learn more about the roles.

You are the alpha in this game. Your job as alpha is to keep your pack healthy and functioning. You make the final call on everything. You can accept new wolves into a pack, decide who and how to fight, deciding when and what to hunt. You will discover many more options and how to use them later in the game.

Warriors fight off intruders. You can train them and they will get stronger. The more warriors you recruit to the pack you the higher probability your hunters will succeed in catching prey

Hunters are what feed the pack. The more hunters you recruit to your pack the higher probability your hunters will succeed in catching prey. Maximum amount of hunters in the pack is 5

When someone is injured from a battle, fight or accident, they are to be sent to healer or others call them Pack doctors. Healers can either be gifted to heal another wolf or are good with medicin and looking after wolves. Healers stay out of pack fights as they are needed to heal, if there are no healers, the pack can slowly grow weak. and GAME OVER. Maximum healers in a pack is 1
